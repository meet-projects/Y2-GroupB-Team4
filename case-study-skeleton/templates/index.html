About

A little about GHIS

Givat Haviva International School in northern Israel is a coed boarding high school, providing an exceptional educational experience. It brings together diverse young students, aged 15 to 18, fostering academic excellence and emotional intelligence for cross-cultural cooperation and conflict resolution. The school values individual backgrounds and personal stories, creating a respectful and listening community. Students live and study together, forming lasting friendships that connect communities. GHIS offers the two-year International Baccalaureate Diploma Programme (IBDP) and a rigorous pre-IB academic education for Israeli and Arab Jewish day students.

the international IB system

  • IB

    system in general

    The International Baccalaureate (IB) system is an internationally recognized education program that aims to develop well-rounded and globally-minded students. It was founded in 1968 and is now offered in schools worldwide.

  • IB

    in GHIS

    At GHIS, we embrace the renowned IB system, empowering well-rounded students for success in a diverse, interconnected world. Our commitment to international-mindedness fosters an inclusive environment, nurturing compassionate global citizens ready to face 21st-century challenges.

  • GHIS

    University Counseling

    The GHIS University Guidance Counselor provides students with information and support throughout the entire university application process: Exploring global universities, programs, and scholarships Supporting application process, essays, and progress monitoring.

Arbel

Jew Israeli

Arbel, from Israel, says that his two years at GHIS taught him that he can apply himself–as a student and a leader. At his previous school, he was bored and unchallenged, barely doing any work. But at GHIS, it was as if he was awakened by the range of interesting and challenging classes available in the IB program.

Hala

Arab Israeli

Hala, from Israel, says that, regarding Palestinians and Jews, “I have my beliefs and they have theirs, and we have the conflict between us, but in the end we just worked it out, we lived together and we also loved each other. You can live with everything, if everyone is on the same page.”
